Senna auriculata (Avaram senna)

	The root is used in decoctions against fevers, diabetes, diseases of urinary system and constipation. The leaves have laxative properties.
	The dried flowers and flower buds are used as a substitute for tea in case of diabetes patients. The powdered seed is also applied to the eye

Uses of Avaram senna :-

	The root is used in decoctions against fevers, diabetes, diseases of urinary system and constipation.

Advantage :-

	Skincare
	Hair growth
	Diabetes
	Pain relief

Disadvantage :-

	Stomach cramps and diarrhoea.
	Stop taking senna if you get diarrhoea.
