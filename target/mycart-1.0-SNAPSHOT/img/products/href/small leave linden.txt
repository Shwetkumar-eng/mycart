USES OF SMALL LEAVED LINDEN(TILIA CORDATA)


     Linden tea has been used in folk medicine across cultures to relieve high blood pressure,

     calm anxiety, and soothe digestion.
 

ADVANTAGE OF SMALL LEAVED linden


     Heart-shaped leaves offer year-round beauty, with seasonal color transformations.

     Fragrant blooms attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, enhancing biodiversity. 

     Natural air conditioner and wildlife haven, providing cool shade and bird shelter.


DISADVANTAGE OF SMALL LEAVED linden


     Linden might cause allergies in some people when taken by mouth.
