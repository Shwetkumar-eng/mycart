Origanum vulgare (Oregano)

	Origanum vulgare) is a species of flowering plant in the mint family Lamiaceae. 
	It was native to the Mediterranean region, 
	but widely naturalised elsewhere in the temperate Northern Hemisphere.

USES OF OREGANO :-

	.Tomato sauces
	.Fried vegetables
	.Grilled meat
	.Turkish choban salad
	.Lemon-olive oil sauce
	.Meat mixtures for burritos and tacos
	.Enchilada sauce
	
ADVANATGE :-

	.Antioxidant
	.Antibacterial
	.Antifungal
	.Expectorant
	.Weight loss

DISADVANATGE :-

	.Abdominal discomfort
	.Heartburn
	.Constipation or diarrhea
	.Nausea and vomiting
	.Dizziness
	.Headache
	.Rash
	.Trouble breathing
	.Inflammation of the airways
