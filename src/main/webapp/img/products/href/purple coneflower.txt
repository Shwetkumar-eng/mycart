USES OF PURPLE CONEFLOWER(ECHINACEA PURPUREA)


    Echinacea, also known as the purple coneflower, is an herbal medicine that has been used for centuries


    customarily as a treatment for the common cold, coughs, bronchitis, upper respiratory infections, and
   
    some inflammatory conditions.



ADVANATGE OF PURPLE CONEFLOWER


        Today people use echinacea to shorten the duration of the common cold and flu, and reduce symptoms,

        such as sore throat (pharyngitis), cough, and fever. Many herbalists also recommend echinacea to help

        boost the immune system and help the body fight infections.

DISADVANTAGE OF PURPLE CONEFLOWER


      The most common side effects of echinacea are digestive tract symptoms, such as nausea or stomach pain. 

      Some people have allergic reactions to echinacea, which may be severe. 

      Some children participating in a clinical trial of echinacea developed rashes, which may have been 

      caused by an allergic reaction.
