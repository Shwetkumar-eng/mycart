Sesuvium portulacastrum (Shoreline purslane)

	The plant extract showed antibacterial and anticandidal activities and moderate antifungal activity.

Uses of Shoreline purslane :-

	 A good source of vitamin C and rich in the naturally occurring steroid, ecdysterone

Advantage :-

	Combats coastal erosion and supports biodiversity with its stabilizing roots.
	Aesthetic and aromatic addition to gardens, with cultural folklore charm.

Disadvantage :-
	
	They can be painful when they pass through your urinary tract. Sometimes, they get stuck and cause more issues.
