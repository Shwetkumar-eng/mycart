USES OF ASTHMA PLANT(EUPHORBIA HIRTA)


  Asthma plants have been used to treat hormone imbalances in women,

  respiratory conditions including asthma, bronchitis, and couthing, and coyza.


ADVANTAGE OF ASTHMA PLANT


  Non-chemical control: Physical control: Control involves hand-pulling plants,

  taking care to remove the entire root system to prevent reshooting.


DISADVANTAGE OF ASTHMA PLANT


  Asthma-plant is mildly toxic, particularly to children.

  Symptoms include nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea when the leaves are ingested raw.
