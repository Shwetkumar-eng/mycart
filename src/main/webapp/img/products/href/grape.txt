USES OF GRAPE(VITIS VINIFERA)


  The nutrients in grapes may help protect against cancer, eye problems, cardiovascular disease,

  and other health conditions


ADVANTAGE OF GRAPE


  Grapes provide both fibre and potassium, which are helpful in their support of heart 

  function including blood pressure.

DISADVANTAGE OF GRAPE
  

  Eating large quantities of grapes might cause diarrhea.
