Syzygium aromaticum (Clove)
	
	Cloves are the aromatic flower buds of a tree in the family Myrtaceae, Syzygium aromaticum .
	They are native to the Maluku Islands, or Moluccas, in Indonesia, and are commonly used as a spice, flavoring, or fragrance in consumer products,
	such as toothpaste, soaps, or cosmetics.
	Cloves are available throughout the year owing to different harvest seasons across various countries.
