USES OF RED CLOVER(TRIFOLIUM PRATENSE)


   It has also been used medicinally to treat several conditions including cancer,

   whooping cough, respiratory problems, and skin inflammations, such as psoriasis and eczema.


ADVANTAGE OF RED CLOVER


   -Red clover may also have blood-thinning properties, which keeps blood clots from forming.


DISADVANTAGE OF RED CLOVER


    Though generally recognized as safe, red clover has minor estrogenic properties

    and should be avoided by those who are pregnant or breastfeeding,

    as well as people with bleeding disorders
