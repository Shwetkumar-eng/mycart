Rosmarinus officinalis (Rosemary)

	Rosemary is an aromatic evergreen shrub with leaves similar to hemlock needles. 
	It is native to the Mediterranean region.
	It can withstand droughts, surviving a severe lack of water for lengthy periods.
	The plant flowers in spring and summer in temperate climates, but the plants can be in constant bloom in warm climates flowers are white, pink, purple or deep blue.

Uses of Rosemary :-

	Rosemary has many uses, including as :
	culinary condiment
	fragrance in cosmetics and soaps, and for its potential health benefits.

Advanatage :-

	.Antimicrobial
	.Anti-inflammatory 
	.Anti-oxidant
	.Anti-apoptotic 
	.Anti-tumorigenic 
	.Antinociceptive and neuroprotective properties

Disadvantage :-

	.Vomiting
	.Spasms
	.Coma
	.Pulmonary edema (fluid in the lungs)
	.Stomach and intestinal irritation
	.Kidney damage
	.Seizures
	.Toxicity
	.Sun sensitivity
	.Skin redness
