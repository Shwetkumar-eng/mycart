NASTURTIUM OFFICINALE (Watercress)

	Watercress or yellowcress (Nasturtium officinale) is a species of aquatic 
	flowering plant in the cabbage family, Brassicaceae.

USES OF WATERCRESS :-

	 Salads
	 Sandwiches
	 Soups
	 Smoothies and garnish

ADVANTAGE :-

	 .Rich in vitamin C, which supports your immune system
 	 .Helps you heal from injuries 
         .Supports healthy collagen production

DISADVANATGE :-

	Stomach or intestinal ulcers
	Kidney disease
	Watercress grown near manure can be a breeding ground for parasites like liver fluke
	Introducing watercress to non-native environments can have negative effects on native species
