USES OF THYME(THYMUS VULGARIS)


   Thyme contains chemicals that might help bacterial and fungal infections.

   It also might help relieve coughing and have antioxidant effects.


ADVANTAGE OF THYME


  - Thyme is particularly rich in vitamin A and vitamin C.


DISADVANTAGE OF THYME


    -It might cause allergic reactions, dizziness, and stomach upset in some people.
