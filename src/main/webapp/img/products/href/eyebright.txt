USES OF EYEBRIGHT(EUPHRASIA)


 Eyebright tightens the mucous membranes of the eye. 



ADVANTAGE OF EYEBRIGHT


  Eyebright is said to be a potential cure for allergies and respiratory symptoms.



DISADVANTAGE OF EYEBRIGHT


  It might cause confusion, headache, nausea, constipation, cough, trouble breathing,

  trouble sleeping (insomnia), and other side effects.
