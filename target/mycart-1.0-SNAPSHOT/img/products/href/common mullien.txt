USES OF COMMON MULLIEN(VERBASCUM THAPSUS)


    Common mullein (Verbascum thapsus L.) is a medicinal plant readily found in roadsides,

    meadows and pasture lands and has been used to treat pulmonary problems, inflammatory diseases,

    asthma  spasmodic coughs, diarrhoea and migraine headaches.


ADVANTAGE OF COMMON MULLIEN


   -  It may also reduce the severity of pain and inflammation in rheumatoid arthritis.


DISADVANTAGE OF COMMON MULLIEN


   Mullein can cause an itchy, allergic rash called contact dermatitis. 

   These side effects are likely because the tiny hairs on the leaves can cause skin irritation.

   These hairs might also irritate your throat and mouth if you drink mullein leaf tea.
