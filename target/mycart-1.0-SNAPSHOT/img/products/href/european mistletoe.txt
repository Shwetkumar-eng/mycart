USES OF EUROPEAN MISTLETOE(VISCUM ALBUM)


  European mistletoe has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for a variety of conditions,

  including seizures, headaches, and menopause symptoms.


ADVANTAGE OF EUROPEAN MISTLETOE


   European mistletoe is promoted as a treatment for cancer.


DISADVANTAGE OF EUROPEAN MISTLETOE


   European mistletoe is probably unsafe for use during pregnancy.
