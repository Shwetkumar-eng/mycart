USES OF VERBENA(VERBENA OFFICINALIS)


    Verbena is used for mild gum disease (gingivitis),

    swelling (inflammation) of the nasal cavity and sinuses (rhinosinusitis),

    heart conditions, depression, and other conditions.


ADVANTAGE OF VERBENA


   Verbena is used for sore throats and respiratory tract diseases

   such as asthma and whooping cough, and for heart conditions such as chest pain (angina) 

   and fluid retention due to heart failure.


DISADVANTAGE OF VERBENA


    verbena might cause sleepiness and drowsiness.
