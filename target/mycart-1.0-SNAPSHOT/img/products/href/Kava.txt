Piper methysticum (Kava)

	Kava or kava kava (Piper methysticum: Latin 'pepper' and Latinized Greek 'intoxicating') is a crop of the Pacific Islands
	Kava is consumed for its sedating effects throughout the Pacific Ocean cultures of Polynesia, including Hawaii and Vanuatu, Melanesia.

Uses of Kava :-

	Treatment of anxiety
	Insomnia and related nervous disorders

Advanatage :-

	.Relieve pain
	.Prevent seizures and relax muscles

Disadvanatge :-

	 .Breathing difficulties
	 .Visual changes 
	 .Including sensitivity to light (photophobia)
	 .Slight alterations to blood cells 
	 .Including white and red blood cells and platelets.
