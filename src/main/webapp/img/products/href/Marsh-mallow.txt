USES of Althaea officinalis(Marsh-mallow)


    It relieves congestion of the chest and irritation of the bladder and lining of

    the stomach which causes painful urination and digestive disorders including diarrhoea. 

    It helps in relieving congestion of the chest which causes breathing difficulties and spasmodic


ADVANTAGE


    Marshmallow is generally considered safe. It has no reported side effects.

    It appears to be safe for use during pregnancy and breastfeeding

    although you should check with your doctor before taking it. One study suggests marshmallow may lower blood sugar levels.


DISADVANTAGE


     Marshmallow might slow blood clotting. Taking marshmallow along with medications

     that also slow blood clotting might increase the risk of bruising and bleeding.
