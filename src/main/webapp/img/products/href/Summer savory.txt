Satureja hortensis (Summer savory)

	Its extracts show antibacterial and antifungal effects on several species including some of the antibiotic resistant strains

Uses of Summer savory :-

	The leaves and stem are used to make medicine. People take summer savory for cough, stomach pain, gas (flatulence), 
	diarrhea, loss of appetite, and many other conditions, but there is no good scientific evidence to support these uses.
	In foods, summer savory is used as a culinary spice.
