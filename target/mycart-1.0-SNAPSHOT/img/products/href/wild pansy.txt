USES OF WILD PANSY(VIOLA TRICOLOR)


 Wild pansy has been used in traditional medicine for centuries for its dermatological effects.


ADVANTAGE OF WILD PANSY


   Wild pansy promotes the disappearance of pimples, helps to improve the general 

   appearance of the skin and helps to maintain a healthy skin.


DISADVANTAGE OF WILD PANSY


   Wild Pansy can lead to an upset stomach. Keep an eye out for nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea.
