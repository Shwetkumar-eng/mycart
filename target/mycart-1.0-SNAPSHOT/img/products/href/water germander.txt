USES OF WATER GERMANDER(TEUCRIUM SCORDIUM)


    Water germander was known from ancient times to have a 'peculiar antiseptic power',

    and was effective 'in all pestilential and putrid diseases'.

ADVANTAGE OF WATER GERMANDER 


     Water germander is a plant. The parts that grow above the ground are used to make medicine.

     People take water germander for asthma, diarrhea, fever, intestinal parasites, hemorrhoids,

     and wounds.
