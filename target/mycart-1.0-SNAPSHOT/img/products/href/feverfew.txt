USES OF FEVERFEW(TANACETUM PARTHENIUM)


   Feverfew (Tanacetum parthenium L.) (Asteraceae) is a medicinal plant traditionally 

   used for the treatment of fevers, migraine headaches, rheumatoid arthritis, stomach aches, 

   toothaches, insect bites, infertility, and problems with menstruation and labor during childbirth.


ADVANTAGE OF FEVERFEW


  - Feverfew is used mostly to treat and prevent headaches.


DISADVANTAGE OF FEVERFEW


    Side effects might include upset stomach, heartburn, diarrhea, constipation, bloating, and vomiting.
