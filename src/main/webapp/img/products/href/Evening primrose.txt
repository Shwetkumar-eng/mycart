OENOTHERA (Evening primrose)

	Oenothera is a genus of about 145 species of herbaceous flowering plants native to the Americas.
	It is the type genus of the family Onagraceae. 
	Common names include evening primrose, suncups, and sundrops. 
	They are not closely related to the true primroses (genus Primula).

USES OF OENOTHERA :-

	Oenothera biennis plant is traditionally used to treat:
	eczema, asthma, rheumatoid arthritis, 
	premenstrual and menopausal syndrome
	and other inflammation-related disorders.

ADVANATGE :-

	.Eczema, acne, and psoriasis
	.Premenstrual syndrome (PMS)
	.Rheumatoid arthritis
	.inflammatory joint conditions
	.Multiple sclerosis symptoms
	.Diabetic neuropathy

DISADVANATGE :-

	.Side effects: EPO can cause nausea, indigestion, headache, diarrhea, and upset stomach.
	.Anticoagulant effects: EPO may increase the anticoagulant effects of other drugs, such as aspirin, warfarin, and heparin.
