USES OF SEXY FOOTPATH LICHEN (XANTHOPAMELIA SCABROSA)


  Xanthoparmelia scabrosa has been marketed as a treatment for erectile dysfunction,

  but many scientists do not recommend this use.

  While the lichen contains a PDE5 inhibitor, which may inhibit an enzyme responsible for impotence,

  the same substance may itself be toxic.


ADVANTAGE OF SEXY FOOTPATH LICHEN


    Lichens in traditional medicine are most commonly used for treating wounds,

    skin disorders, respiratory and digestive issues, and obstetric and gynecological concerns.


DISADVANTAGE OF SEXY FOOTHPATH LICHEN

    
      A few people have been known to have allergic reactions to lichens, resulting in skin disorders.
