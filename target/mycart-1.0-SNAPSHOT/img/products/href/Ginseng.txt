Panax (Ginseng)

	The Panax (ginseng) genus belongs to the Araliaceae family.
	 Panax species are characterized by the presence of ginsenosides and gintonin. 

USES OF PANAX :-

	 Panax ginseng is taken by mouth for memory and thinking skills,
	 Alzheimer disease, depression, and many other conditions.

ADVANTAGE :-

	.Boost energy 
	.Lower blood sugar and cholesterol levels
	.Reduce stress
	.Promote relaxation
	.Treat diabetes
	.Manage sexual dysfunction in men

DISADVANTAGE :-

	.Immune disorders
	.Bleeding conditions
	.Cardiovascular diseases and cancer.
