ILLICIUM VERUM(Star anise)

	Illicium verum (star anise or badian, Chinese star anise, star anise seed, star aniseed and star of anise)
        is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to northeast Vietnam and South China. It is a spice that closely resembles anise in flavor and is obtained from the star-shaped pericarps of the fruit of I. 
        verum which are harvested just before ripening. 

USES OF ILLICIUM VERUM :-

	 Star anise oil is a highly fragrant oil used in
	 cooking, perfumery, soaps, toothpastes, mouthwashes, and skin creams.
	 Star anise enhances the flavor of meat.

ADVANTAGE :-

		.Antioxidant
		.Antimicrobial
		.Antifungal
		.Anthelmintic
		.Antinociceptive
		.Anti-inflammatory
		.Gastroprotective
		.Expectorant and spasmolytic, and estrogenic effects.

DISADVANTAGE :-

		.Star anise can be toxic to young children, causing neurological and gastrointestinal symptoms.
		.Star anise can cause serious side effects when used in large doses.
		.Star anise tea have shown irritability, vomiting, and seizures.
