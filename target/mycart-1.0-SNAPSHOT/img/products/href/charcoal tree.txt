USES OF CHARCOAL TREE (TREMA ORIENTALIS)


    Charcoal tree is mainly used in agroforestry.


ADVANTAGE OF CHARCOAL TREE


    The calorific value of charcoal is more than wood. 


DISADVANTAGE OF CHARCOAL TREE


    Common side effects include constipation and black stools.

    When applied to the skin: Activated charcoal is likely safe for most adults when applied to wounds.
