USES of citrus trifoliata(Orange)


    Bitter orange has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for indigestion,

    nausea, and constipation. Today, various bitter orange products are promoted for heartburn,

    nasal congestion, weight loss, appetite stimulation or suppression, and athletic performance.


ADVANTAGE


    By killing germs deep in the pores and reducing excess oil,

    Bitter Orange Oil helps treat existing breakouts and prevent new blemishes from forming,

    which leads to smoother, clearer skin.


DISADVANTAGE


     Bitter orange, taken by itself or with stimulants such as caffeine or caffeine-containing herbs,

     might increase the risk for high blood pressure, fainting, heart attack, stroke, and other serious side effects.

     There are also reports that bitter orange can trigger headaches in some people.
