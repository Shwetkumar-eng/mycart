USES of Apium Graveolens(Celery)


    Graveolens are used in hepatic and spleen disorders, brain disorders, body pain, and sleep disturbances.


ADVANTAGE


   1: Improves sex life

   2: Improves sperm count and activity

   3: Reduces cancer risk

   4: Lowers blood pressure level

   5: Prevents inflammation

   6: Promotes nerve cells health and prevent memory loss

   7: Aids digestion

   8: Helps to reduce weight.


DISADVANTAGE


    The compounds such as psoralen, xanthotoxin and bergapten are found in seeds and leaves of celery. 

    These are phototoxic, which, when coming in contact with the sunlight, get more concentrated and 

    may cause dermatitis in humans and animals.
