Uses of Carica papaya(Papaya)

     Papaya are a sweet fruit and grow in tropical regions. 

     They have many varieties. Papaya has many benefits,

     including protection against heart disease, reduced inflammation, aid in digestion, and boosting your immune system.


ADVANTAGE


    The possible health benefits of consuming papaya include a reduced risk of heart disease    

    diabetes, cancer, aiding in digestion, improving blood glucose control in people with diabetes

    lowering blood pressure, and improving wound healing.


DISADVANTAGE


   . Skin irritation

   . Allergic reaction

   . Uterine contractions

   . Carotenemia 

   . Stomach upset
