USES OF VALERIAN(VALERIANA OFFICINALIS)


   - valerian was used to treat insomnia, migraine, fatigue, and stomach cramps.


ADVANTAGE OF VALERIAN


    valerian is promoted for insomnia, anxiety, depression, premenstrual syndrome (PMS), 

    menopause symptoms, and headaches.


DISADVANTAGE OF VALERIAN


    Although valerian is thought to be fairly safe, side effects such as headache,

    dizziness, stomach problems or sleeplessness may occur.
