Stellaria media (Common chickweed)

	Stellaria media, chickweed, is an annual flowering plant in the family Caryophyllaceae. 
	It is native to Eurasia and naturalized throughout the world, where it is a weed of waste ground, farmland and gardens. 
	It is sometimes grown as a salad crop or for poultry consumption.

Uses of Common chickweed :-

	Chickweed is used to treat a variety of ailments, including skin problems, bladder and kidney issues, and urinary tract problems
	Chickweed can be eaten as food and added to many dishes, such as salads, sandwiches.
	Chickweed can be mixed with other greens like spinach or kale in salads, added to sandwiches or wraps, 
	or used as a substitute for other greens like Swiss chard, mustard greens, or collard greens

Advantage :-
	
	Skin conditions
	Cooling and anti-inflammatory properties
	Respiratory tract illnesses

Disadvantage :-
	
	Nausea
	Upset stomach
	Diarrhea and vomiting
	Skin irritation
