USES OF ASHWAGANDHA(WITHANIA SOMNIFERA)


   It is useful for different types of diseases like Parkinson, dementia, memory loss,

   stress induced diseases, malignoma and others.


ADVANTAGE OF ASHWAGANDHA


   Ashwagandha contains chemicals that might help calm the brain, reduce swelling,

   lower blood pressure, and alter the immune system.


DISADVANTAGE OF ASHWANGANDHA


   Ashwagandha might cause sleepiness and slowed breathing.
