USES of cissampelos pareira(velvetleaf)


    The Chinese also used the plant for medicinal purposes to treat fever, dysentery, stomachaches and other problems.


ADVANTAGE


    The plant is also used to make ropes, coarse cloth, nets, paper,

    and caulk for boats, and is still cultivated in China to this day. 

    Velvetleaf is used in Chinese traditional medicine as a treatment for

    dysentery and to treat eye injuries, including opacity of the cornea.
