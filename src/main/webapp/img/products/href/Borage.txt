USES of Borago officinalis(Borage) 


      Borago officinalis has been used in hyperactive gastrointestinal, respiratory and cardiovascular disorders

      such as gastrointestinal (colic, cramps, diarrhea), airways (asthma, bronchitis), cardiovascular

      (cardiotonic, antihypertensive and blood purifier), urinary (diuretic and kidney/bladder disorders)


ADVANTAGE


   Borage flower and leaves are used for fever, cough, and depression. 

   Borage is also used for a hormone problem called adrenal insufficiency, 

   for "blood purification," to increase urine flow, to prevent inflammation of the lungs, as a sedative,

   and to promote sweating.


DISADVANTAGE


    . Nausea

    . Indigestion

    . Headaches

    . Rashes
