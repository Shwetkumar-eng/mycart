USES OF CHASTEBERRY(VITEX AGNUS CASTUS)


   Chaste tree has been used to treat menstrual cycle problems and pain,

   premenstrual syndrome, and menopause.


ADVANTAGE OF CHASTEBEERY


  chasteberry is promoted as a dietary supplement for symptoms of premenstrual syndrome,

  breast pain associated with the menstrual cycle, infertility, and other condition.


DISADVANTAGE OF CHASTEBERRY


   Chasteberry can affect levels of hormones that play a key role in pregnancy,

   breastfeeding, menstruation, and even some breast cancers.
