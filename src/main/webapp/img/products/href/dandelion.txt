ADVANTAGE OF DANDELION

     Fresh or dried dandelion herb is also used as a mild appetite stimulant, and to 

     improve upset stomach.


DISADVANTAGE OF DANDELION

     In some people consuming a lot of dandelion can have a negative effect on health.

     In particular, this can impact people living with diabetes or taking medications 
 
     like blood thinners, lithium, or diuretics.
