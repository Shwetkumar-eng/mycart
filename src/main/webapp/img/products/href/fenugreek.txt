USES OF FENUGREEK(TRIGONELLA FOENUM GRACEUM)


    Fenugreek is used as an ingredient in spice blends and a flavoring agent in foods,

    beverages, and tobacco. 

    Fenugreek extracts are also used in soaps and cosmetics.


ADVANTAGE OF FENUGREEK

     Fenugreek is native to the Mediterranean, Europe, and Asia.

     Fenugreek seems to slow sugar absorption in the stomach and stimulate insulin.


DISADVANTAGE OF FENUGREEK


    It should not be used by children as a supplement.

    Potential side effects of fenugreek include diarrhea, nausea, 

    and other digestive tract symptoms and rarely, dizziness and headaches.
